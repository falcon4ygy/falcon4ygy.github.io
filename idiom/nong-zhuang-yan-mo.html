浓妆艳抹是我的心意

Nóngzhuāngyànmǒ shì wǒ de xīnyì

To dress flamboyantly is my intention

In this lyric, the singer claims that their intent is to dress lavishly, flaunting their wealth and setting a standard for what should be considered "beautiful".
