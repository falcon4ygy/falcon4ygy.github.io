赴汤蹈火

fù tāng dǎo huǒ

literal meaning: swim through boiling water and tread on fire
translated: to go to great pains (for the sake of something)

赴

fù

swim

汤

tāng

boiling water, soup

蹈

dǎo

tread, step on

火

huǒ

fire

Examples

买辣椒也用券 - 起风了

Feng Qinyuan - Qi Feng Le

赴汤蹈火去走它一遍

Yě gānyuàn fùtāng-dǎohuǒ qù zǒu tā yībiàn

And willing to face any difficulties to reach there

In this lyric, the singer describes the youthful determination they felt earlier in their life. Being willing to "walk through fire and swim through boiling water" is a metaphor for being ready to face any difficulties without hesitation.
